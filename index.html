N-Queens Project

N-Queens Solver

The N-Queens Solver is an advanced algorithm implemented in Python to address the classical N-Queens problem. This solution employs Monte Carlo simulations to efficiently manage large instances, such as the 12-queens problem, executing up to 100 independent simulation runs. The algorithm meticulously places queens on the chessboard, ensuring that no two queens are in a position to threaten each other, while leveraging randomization techniques to explore diverse configurations and substantially minimize the search space. With a demonstrated accuracy rate exceeding 95%, the solver effectively traverses thousands of nodes per simulation, consistently yielding optimal solutions. Additionally, the project encompasses comprehensive visualization tools, including detailed board configuration displays and histograms of nodes visited, offering valuable insights into the algorithm's operational performance and efficiency. This robust approach underscores the solver's capability to deliver precise and reliable results in solving complex combinatorial problems.
